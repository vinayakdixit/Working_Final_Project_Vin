Machine Learning Models

We utilized the following 7 machine learning classification models: K Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Multinomial Naive Bayes, Neural Network, XG Boost, Support Vector Classifier (SVC), Logistic Regression, and Random Forest. To check the accuracy of each model, we have included the ROC Curve, a table with Classification Report data, and the Confusion Matrix. The models are in order based on their AUC Score. The AUC is the area under the ROC curve. This score gives us a good idea of how well the model performs.
